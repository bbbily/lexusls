    Since the first LS was launched in 1989, Lexus has never slowed in its
    relentless pursuit of perfection.

    Each LS generation set new industry standards and showcased
    class-leading excellence.

  • 1989

    LS 400

    It displayed superb engine performance and gained global admiration and approval. This

    was a level of design, performance and quality that no other vehicle could match up to.

  • 1994
